## 🛠️ How to Use

After downloading **orchestra**, follow these steps to get started:

1. **Installation:**
   - Locate the downloaded file in your downloads folder.
   - Double-click the file to start the installation process.
   - Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the setup.

2. **Launching the Application:**
   - Once installed, locate **orchestra** in your applications menu or desktop.
   - Click on the icon to open the application.

3. **Adding Music:**
   - Click on the "Add Music" button to browse your files.
   - Select the music files or folders you want to add.
   - Click "Open" to add them to **orchestra**.

4. **Creating a Playlist:**
   - Go to the "Playlists" section.
   - Click "New Playlist."
   - Name your playlist and add songs to it.

5. **Playing Music:**
   - Select any song from your list.
   - Use the play button to start listening.
   - Adjust volume and other settings conveniently.
